# Workbench

The Workbench answers:

> What are we currently thinking about?

This is the exploratory area of Spark OS. Content here may be incomplete, contradictory, speculative, or awaiting a decision.

## What Belongs Here

- early ideas
- drafts
- unresolved questions
- alternative structures
- architectural debates
- observations that may become improvements
- work awaiting subject-matter validation

## What Does Not Belong Here

- accepted mortgage knowledge
- active operational standards
- finalized course content
- authoritative AI instructions
- retired material that has already been replaced

## Structure

- [`architect-log`](architect-log/) — observations about how Spark OS is evolving
- [`decisions`](decisions/) — decision proposals and reasoning before acceptance
- [`drafts`](drafts/) — incomplete content
- [`parking-lot`](parking-lot/) — worthwhile ideas not yet scheduled

## Graduation Rule

Workbench content graduates only when its purpose, destination, and authority are clear.

Possible destinations include Philosophy, Governance, Canon, or Academy.

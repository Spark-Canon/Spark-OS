# Vision

Spark-OS will become the operating system for a modern mortgage brokerage.

It should connect client relationships, applications, documents, underwriting, workflows, communication, compliance, reporting, training, and integrations in one coherent operational model.

Spark-OS should reduce fragmented software, duplicated data, missed responsibilities, and undocumented institutional knowledge.

The system should make the correct next action visible, assign responsibility clearly, preserve the reasoning behind decisions, and allow humans and AI to collaborate safely.

## Intended Outcomes

- One coherent system of record
- Clear ownership of work
- Configurable workflows
- Traceable business decisions
- Strong privacy and compliance boundaries
- Reusable institutional knowledge
- AI assistance grounded in repository and business context
- Reduced dependence on disconnected subscriptions

# ADR-003: Causes Over Outcomes

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-07-20

## Context

Metrics describe results but do not directly control the operational causes that produce those results.

## Decision

Spark-OS should manage controllable causes: responsibilities, actions, workflows, evidence, decisions, and follow-up.

## Consequences

- Reporting remains derived from operations.
- Dashboards should not replace workflow design.
- Product decisions should prioritize operational leverage.

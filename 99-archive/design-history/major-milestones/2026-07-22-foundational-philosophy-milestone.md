## Milestone

Spark OS installed its first accepted foundational Philosophy for Spark Mortgages: the Manifesto and five Values. The milestone also established Explore → Challenge → Audit → Close → Distill as a proportionate governing work method inside the existing repository lifecycle.

## Why It Mattered

The repository previously contained the Philosophy structure and document boundaries but not accepted substantive foundations. This milestone converted extensive exploration into concise canonical documents while preserving the distinction between exploratory reasoning, human approval, bounded implementation, and independent verification.

The work demonstrated that detailed exploration can inform canonical simplicity without creating additional Philosophy documents, authorities, knowledge states, or boot layers.

- five unranked Values in one canonical document
- the governing Explore → Challenge → Audit → Close → Distill method in `CONTRIBUTING.md`
